What the data confirms

  • Delivery rate held at 90–94% across all 5 monthsAll monthly reports
  • Click rate was consistently 3% every month — no movement despite volume growthAll monthly reports

Confirmed flags

  • Several campaigns had 0 revenue despite strong open ratesPer-campaign reports
Recommended Actions
Based on data
Urgent

List hygiene audit. April recorded 4,453 bounces (13% bounce rate). Must be addressed before next high-volume send.

Source: April per-campaign reports

Open Rate

Feb open rate was 20%, 12pts below industry average. Review subject lines and send timing for those 4 campaigns.

Source: Feb aggregate · industry avg ~32%

Click→Revenue

Several campaigns had strong open rates but zero revenue (Ice Industry: 43.6% open, $0). Review CTA and landing page alignment.

Source: May per-campaign reports

Segment

Industry Update emails consistently outperformed product emails on revenue. Consider increasing frequency or list targeting.

Source: Mar, Apr, May reports

Top Content

FBK Buckets (6.2% CR, $8,563), Toolflex (3.4% CR, $15,044), and Industry Update (2.5% CR, $35,921) were March star performers.

Source: March per-campaign reports

Desktop

100% of clicks in May and ~98% in Mar/Apr came from desktop. Mobile-first design is less critical for CTR — but opens are ~10–30% mobile.

Source: All heatmap reports
