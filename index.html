Recommended actions Based on data
Urgent

List hygiene audit. April recorded 4,453 bounces (13% bounce rate). This must be addressed before next high-volume send.

Source: April per-campaign reports

Open rate

Feb open rate was 20%, 12 pts below industry average. Review subject lines and send timing for those 4 campaigns.

Source: Feb aggregate · industry avg ~32%

Click→Revenue

Several campaigns had strong open rates but zero revenue (Ice Industry: 43.6% open, $0 revenue). Review CTA and landing page alignment.

Source: May per-campaign reports

Segment

Industry Update emails consistently outperformed product emails on revenue. Consider increasing frequency or list targeting for this format.

Source: Mar, Apr, May reports

Top content

FBK Buckets (6.2% CR, $8,563), Toolflex (3.4% CR, $15,044), and Industry Update (2.5% CR, $35,921) were March star performers.

Source: March per-campaign reports

Desktop

100% of clicks in May and ~98% in Mar/Apr came from desktop. Mobile-first design is less critical for click-through — but opens are ~10–30% mobile.

Source: All heatmap reports
